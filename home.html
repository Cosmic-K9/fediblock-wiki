Welcome to The #FEDIBLOCK Wiki!

If you have any questions, concerns or comments, please feel free to reach out to us at @the-fediblock-wiki.

Have an instance you'd like to see here? Email us at submissions@thefediblock.wiki.

If you are an operator of an instance that appears on this wiki, and would like to appeal our decision, please email us at appeals@thefediblock.wiki. It may take up to 72 hours to receive a response, so have patience.

This wiki should not be used as a definitive list of federated services ("instances") to defederate from.
Administrators of instances viewing this wiki should consider the things listed on this wiki, and whether the instance is deserving of a defederation before doing so.

We do not tolerate the harassment of other instances into defederating from another, risking defederation of the other instances if they don't.
If an instance is caught doing this, they have a high probability of showing up here. This is highly-irresponsible behaviour and only serves to destroy the concept of federation in the first place. The choice to defederate from an instance lies squarely with the administrators and operators of any given instance.

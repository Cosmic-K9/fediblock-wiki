[ Special ] mastodon.lol

mastodon.lol, according to it's “About” page as of February 10, is “A Mastodon server friendly towards anti-fascists, members of the LGBTQ+ community, hackers, and the like.”

Instance shutdown

On February 9th, 2023 at 12:20pm mastodon.lol timezone,  the lead administrator and operator of mastodon.lol sent a message out on their account. This message informed their users that on May 9, 2023 that the mastodon.lol instance would be shutting down.

“…Mastodon.lol will shut down on May 9, 2023, 3 months from today…”

The reason @nathan@mastodon.lol provided is as follows:

“…I'm not doing this anymore. It's not worth it. Personal attacks calling me of all people a Nazi, a TERF, an antisemite...”

This is possibly in relation to a previous message they sent out on the same account, informing their users that they would not be banning users for playing Hogwarts Legacy, a game based on the books written by writer, author and infamous transgender-exclusionary feminist (TERF) J.K. Rowling.

"We're not banning people for buying a video game. Express your displeasure to them if you so wish, but that alone doesn't require our attention.

Related, please don't post spoilers just to spite people. You can justify it to yourself however you like, but you're shouting into a crowd here. Not every HP fan knows J.K. Rowling is a piece of shit, and ruining the days of random bystanders ain't the way to go.

It *will* cause problems for myself and .lol in general if it continues. Thanks."

Link to message - Archived Link

Many of the comments under this message are users of the instance, upset that @nathan was feigning ignorance, and by advocating for a more civil way of protesting a game, the instance operator was speaking from a place of privilege.

Final statements

We at The #FEDIBLOCK Wiki would like to commend @nathan@mastodon.lol for running their instance as well as they have for as long as they have. It's never easy to appease to both sides of an argument. No matter what, the other side will feel betrayed. At the end of the day, it's about making decisions that will benefit the instance as a whole.

However, if the screenshot of @nathan instructing their critics to commit suicide is real, we do have to step up and condemn this behaviour. While it is understandable that they were under immense stress, this does not excuse one from making such statements.

Since @nathan does appear to be offline for the foreseeable future, it is difficult to tell if we will hear anymore from them in regards to this situation. Nathan, we would like to hear your side of the story, so feel free to DM or email us.
